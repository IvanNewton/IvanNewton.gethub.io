About Me

I'm Ivan Newton, a digital artist interested in working on interactive experiences and narratives. I enjoy making artistic projects that are eye catching and unique. Graduating from the University of California, Santa Cruz with a Bachelor's degree in Art and Design for Game and Playable Media and Film and Digital Media, I'm passionate about creating and designing aspects for games digital and physical. While in university I worked with various mediums like card games, board games, and video games. My interests don't just end with games though — I am also passionate about animation and film. I have always been drawn to the intersection of design and creation ever since I was young, and I consider myself a perpetual student of coding and software development.
